# Spec: Looping Video Player

## GIVEN a screen recording video file (MP4, 1080p) exists
## WHEN `scripts/siam-video-player.html` is opened in a browser
### THEN the video plays fullscreen, loops automatically, and has no visible controls
### AND pressing F11 toggles true fullscreen mode

## GIVEN the video is playing in the browser
## WHEN a visitor at SIAM sees the second screen
### THEN they see the AgroGina demo walkthrough with on-screen French text annotations
### AND the video seamlessly loops with no visible gap
